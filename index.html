App Description:
Terp Pro is an application in which students can sign up to get in touch with other students whos specialze in different fields. Thes students are called "Pros." The Pros are selected via interviews with the admin, us and we can verify their skillsets through assigning them tasks. The Pro list will be updated by us every semester, so users can get different experiences.

Users can only sign up if and only if they are students at the University of Maryland. We verify their identity through the email ending witn "terpmail.umd.edu" and these users will be able to sign in and sign up through the app, select the Pros they prefer and give them reviews, so in the future they can select only out of the Pros that they have good experience with.

We have intentionally got rid of the shared review, because reading different potentially negative reviews of themselves can negatively affect Pros, who are also students.
